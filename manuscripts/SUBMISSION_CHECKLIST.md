# Journal Submission Checklist

## Manuscript

- [ ] Title page complete

- [ ] Keywords selected (usually 3-6)

- [ ] References formatted correctly
- [ ] Supplementary materials organized

## Figures Required

### Figure 1: Discovery Rates Bar Chart
- Data: `figure_data.json` → `figure1_discovery_rates`
- Scripts: `scripts/figure1_discovery_rates.R` or `.py`
- Shows: Percentage of significant clock-target pairs by tissue

### Figure 2: Circadian Gating Heatmap
- Data: `figure_data.json` → `figure2_heatmap`
- Scripts: `scripts/figure2_heatmap.R` or `.py`
- Shows: -log10(p-value) for significant pairs across tissues

### Figure 3: Tissue Vulnerability Model (Schematic)
- Data: `figure_data.json` → `figure3_pathway_model`
- Script: `scripts/figure3_model.py` (basic) or use BioRender
- Shows: Conceptual model of tissue-specific gating
